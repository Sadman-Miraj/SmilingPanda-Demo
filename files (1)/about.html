How it started

It began with one very good mug

In late 2025, we were hunting for a gift that was affordable, well-made, and actually joyful — and kept finding the same tired options everywhere. So we designed our own mug at a kitchen table, ordered a tiny batch, and gave the leftovers away online.

They disappeared in a weekend. People asked for more. Smiling Panda became a real shop in January 2026, and we've kept the same rule ever since: if it doesn't make us grin during testing, it doesn't get made.

We're still small — two humans, one studio, a lot of bubble-wrap-free packing paper — and we like it that way.
