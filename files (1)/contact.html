FAQ

Quick answers

The five questions we get asked the most.

How long does shipping take?

Orders placed before 3pm ship the same working day. Standard delivery takes 2–4 business days locally, and 5–10 business days internationally. You'll get a tracking link the moment your parcel leaves the studio.

When is shipping free?

Every order over $40 ships free, automatically — no code needed. Under $40, standard shipping is a flat $4.50.

What's your return policy?

You have 30 days from delivery to return anything, in any condition, for a full refund. We cover the return postage, and there are no forms or awkward questions — we call it the Smile Guarantee.

Is gift wrapping really free?

Yes! Every order arrives wrapped in tissue paper with a ribbon and a handwritten note. Ordering a gift? Leave a message at checkout and we'll write it on the card for you.

Do you do wholesale?

We love working with small shops and cafés. Email hello@smilingpanda.shop with "Wholesale" in the subject and a little about your store, and we'll send over our line sheet.
